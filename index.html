About Basti Ki Pathshala

We are a community-driven organization with a passionate team dedicated to making a difference.

Our Story

"Basti Ki Pathshala" started in 2018 with a simple mission: to ensure that every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education. What began in a small rented room with 15 children has now grown into a thriving learning center supporting over 200 students.

Our dedicated team of teachers and volunteers works tirelessly to create a safe, inclusive, and inspiring space for our children to learn and grow.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education and holistic development opportunities to underprivileged children, empowering them to build a brighter future.

Our Vision

A world where every child has the support and resources they need to reach their full potential and break the cycle of poverty.
